FDA Deeming Authority Clarification Act of 2015

Introduced by Representative Tom Cole of Oklahoma, HR 2058 would save the U.S. vapor industry from being destroyed by improper regulations by the FDA.

This bill would amend the Food, Drug, and Cosmetics Act to change the grandfather date for “deemed tobacco products.” This change would allow for all vapor products currently on the market to remain on the market without being subject to the burdensome (read: prohibitive) pre-market FDA approval application process.
